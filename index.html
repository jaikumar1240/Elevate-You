Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Jai Rajput. I help students and young professionals develop practical skills in dressing, communication, and social interactions. My approach focuses on real-world application rather than abstract concepts.

100+ Students Transformed

I work primarily with college students and recent graduates who are navigating career transitions, interviews, and building their professional identity.

Improved Dressing sense

I help you understand how to dress appropriately for different occasions—from college presentations to job interviews—while staying within your budget.

Social Skills Mastery

I provide feedback on how you present yourself in conversations, interviews, and social settings, helping you express yourself more effectively.

Why I Do This

I remember what it felt like to struggle with confidence, especially when it came to interviews and social situations. Over time, I learned practical approaches to dressing, speaking, and interacting that made a real difference. Now, I share what I've learned to help others avoid some of the mistakes I made and build their confidence faster.
